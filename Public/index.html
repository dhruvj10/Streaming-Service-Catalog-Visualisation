Movies Produced in Each Genre Every 500 Years

This visualization uses a stacked area chart to show how different genres of movies have evolved over time. Each layer represents a genre, and the chart provides insights into the historical popularity and production trends of various genres across centuries.

Director Contributions: Number of Movies Produced

The bubble chart highlights the contribution of directors to the movie industry. Each bubble represents a director, with its size indicating the total number of movies produced by that director. This visualization emphasizes the impact of prolific directors in shaping the industry.

Stacked Bar Chart: Cumulative Count of Movies and TV Shows by Type and Rating

This stacked bar chart breaks down the distribution of movies and TV shows by their rating categories. It allows users to compare the prevalence of different content types and ratings, such as PG-13, TV-MA, or R-rated content.

Treemap of Movie Genres Produced Till 2020

The treemap visually represents the proportion of movies produced in each genre up to 2020. The size of each rectangle reflects the number of movies in a genre, offering an at-a-glance understanding of which genres dominate the movie landscape.
